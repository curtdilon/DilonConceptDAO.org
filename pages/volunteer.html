Free Dilonland DAO Citizenship

All volunteers receive free citizenship in the Dilonland DAO virtual country, represented as an NFT on the blockchain.

As a citizen, you'll be able to:

  • Participate in governance decisions
  • Access exclusive resources and educational content
  • Join virtual events and community discussions
  • Build your own virtual Dilon House
  • Earn the opportunity to become a Captain Dilon with voting rights

Volunteer FAQs

How much time do I need to commit as a volunteer?

+

We welcome volunteers with various levels of availability. You can contribute as little as a few hours per month or as much as 20+ hours per week, depending on your schedule and the projects you're involved with. We also offer project-based volunteering opportunities for those who prefer to work on specific initiatives.

Can I volunteer remotely?

+

Yes! Most of our volunteer opportunities can be done remotely from anywhere in the world. We use various digital tools to coordinate and collaborate with our global volunteer team.

How do I receive my Dilonland DAO citizenship?

+

After your volunteer application is approved and you've completed your first volunteer task, you'll receive instructions on how to claim your Dilonland DAO citizenship. This typically involves creating an account on the Dilonland platform and connecting a digital wallet to receive your citizenship NFT. If you're not familiar with blockchain technology, don't worry - we'll guide you through the process.

Can organizations volunteer their services?

+

Absolutely! We welcome partnerships with organizations that want to contribute their expertise and resources. Organizations that volunteer their services will be featured on our Partners page and receive special recognition in the Dilonland DAO. Please indicate in your application that you're applying as an organization.
