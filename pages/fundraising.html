Why Your Support Matters

The Dilon Concept Foundation is working to create a more equitable society where every person has access to their fair share of Earth's resources. Your donation directly supports:

Research & Development

Funding research into resource-based economic models and developing the Democracy 2.0 platform.

Dilon House Prototypes

Building and testing self-sufficient Dilon House prototypes to demonstrate sustainable living.

Educational Programs

Developing curriculum for Dilon Schools and educational materials about resource-based economics.

Advocacy & Outreach

Spreading awareness about the Dilon Concept and advocating for resource rights for all.

Donation FAQs

Is my donation tax-deductible?

+

The Dilon Concept Foundation is in the process of obtaining 501(c)(3) status. Once approved, donations will be tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law. We recommend consulting with a tax professional regarding your specific situation.

How will my donation be used?

+

Your donation directly supports the development and implementation of the Dilon Concept, including research, prototype development, educational programs, and advocacy efforts. We maintain transparent financial reporting and provide regular updates to our supporters on how funds are being utilized.

Can I donate if I'm outside the United States?

+

Yes! We welcome support from around the world. Our GoFundMe campaign accepts international donations, and our direct donation system supports multiple currencies.

How do I receive my Dilonland DAO citizenship?

+

After your donation is processed, you'll receive an email with instructions on how to claim your Dilonland DAO citizenship. This typically involves creating an account on the Dilonland platform and connecting a digital wallet to receive your citizenship NFT.
